Linkin Park
American rock band founded in 1996. Existing since 2000 under the name Linkin Park, the band has been nominated six times and twice received a Grammy Award. The band found success thanks to the debut album in 2000 called Hybrid Theory, which sold more than 30 million copies
Black Veil Brides
American metal band from Ohio, founded in 2006. A distinctive feature of the band is an unusual stage image inspired by Kiss, Mötley Crüe and other glam metal artists of the 1980s.
Green Day
American rock band formed in East Bay, California in 1987 by vocalist and guitarist Billie Joe Armstrong along with bassist and backing vocalist Mike Dernt. Green Day has sold over 75 million copies of records worldwide. The band has been nominated for 20 Grammy Awards
Evanescence
American rock band founded in 1995 by vocalist Amy Lee and guitarist Ben Moody. The band achieved great popularity in early 2003 with the release of the album Fallen, which sold about 15 million copies and brought the band two Grammy Awards
Bring Me The Horizon
Is a British rock band from Sheffield, Yorkshire. The group was created in 2004 by members of disbanded bands from their area: they got their name based on a phrase from the movie Pirates of the Caribbean
Three Days Grace
Canadian rock band performing alternative metal and post-grunge. It was formed under the name Groundswell in Norwood, Ontario, Canada in 1992. In 1997, the band, consisting of guitarist and vocalist Adam Gontier, drummer Neil Sanderson and bassist Brad Wolst, changed its name to the current one.
Skillet
American Christian rock band from Memphis, Tennessee, founded in 1996. At the moment, the band has released eleven studio albums, four EP's and two live albums. The band consists of John Cooper and his wife Corey Cooper, as well as Jen Ledger, Seth Morrison.
Simple Plane
Canadian rock band formed in Montreal at the end of 1999. All the members of the quintet are French Canadians, born in the province of Quebec. The group got its name in honor of the 1998 movie "Simple Plan". At the moment, the band has released five studio albums and three live albums.
